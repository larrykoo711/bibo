Bibo

Your text. Your voice. Your machine.

100% LOCAL NEURAL TTS OPEN SOURCE ‚ö† MOSTLY HARMLESS

Click to experience neural TTS magic

In the age of cloud everything, your words travel thousands of miles just to become sound. They pass through servers, get logged, analyzed, maybe even listened to.

Bibo believes your voice should stay yours.

A tiny neural network lives on your machine. It reads your text. It speaks. No internet required. No accounts. No subscriptions. No limits. Just you and your words.

// How it works

  • bibo "The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog."

    Speak text instantly with neural voice

  • bibo "Welcome to Bibo." -v ryan

    Switch voices with -v flag

  • bibo -d amy

    Download voice models (~50MB each)

  • cat essay.txt | bibo -o reading.wav

    Pipe text, save to file. Unix philosophy.

  • bibo -f "Ship it!"

    Fast mode. 1.2x speed. For the impatient.
